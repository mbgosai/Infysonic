Our 5-Phase Delivery Process

Average time from kick-off to working Proof of Concept: 4–6 weeks. No slide decks before delivery.

01
Discovery & Data Assessment (Weeks 1–2)
Deep dive into business objectives, data quality, feature availability, and historical patterns. Deliver prioritized use cases with data readiness score.
02
Data Engineering & Feature Store (Weeks 2–4)
Clean, integrate, and engineer features. Build reproducible feature pipelines and establish baseline models using your real data.
03
Model Development & Validation (Weeks 3–7)
Train, tune, and rigorously validate multiple models. Include explainability analysis, fairness checks, and backtesting against historical outcomes.
04
Production Deployment & Integration (Weeks 6–14)
Deploy models with monitoring, A/B testing, API endpoints, and real-time scoring. Full integration with business systems and dashboards.
05
Monitor, Retrain & Scale (Ongoing)
Model performance monitoring, drift detection, automated retraining pipelines, and continuous business value reporting.
