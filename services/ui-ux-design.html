Our 5-Phase Delivery Process

Average time from kick-off to validated high-fidelity prototype: 4–7 weeks.

01
Research & Discovery (Weeks 1–2)
Stakeholder workshops, user interviews, competitive analysis, and journey mapping with clear opportunity areas.
02
Information Architecture & Wireframes (Weeks 2–4)
Sitemap, user flows, low-fidelity wireframes, and early usability testing for validation.
03
Visual Design & Prototyping (Weeks 3–6)
High-fidelity designs, interactive prototypes, component library, and accessibility reviews.
04
User Testing & Iteration (Weeks 5–8)
Moderated testing sessions, A/B variants, and data-driven refinement of key flows.
05
Design System & Handoff (Ongoing)
Complete component library, Figma-to-code handoff, documentation, and design QA support.
