Our 5-Phase Delivery Process

Average time from kick-off to working Proof of Concept: 4–6 weeks. No slide decks before delivery.

01
Discovery & AI Readiness Assessment (Weeks 1–2)
Deep evaluation of your data quality, infrastructure, team capabilities, and business goals. You receive a scored readiness report with prioritized opportunities.
02
AI Strategy & Roadmap (Weeks 2–3)
Rank use cases by ROI and feasibility. Deliver a phased 12–36 month roadmap with success metrics, data requirements, skill gaps, and budget ranges.
03
Proof of Concept Build (Weeks 3–6)
Build a working PoC using your real data. Includes accuracy benchmarks, latency targets, and clear read on business impact before any larger investment.
04
Production Build & Deployment (Weeks 6–16)
Full engineering, CI/CD, security review, integration, performance benchmarking, and user acceptance testing before go-live.
05
Optimize, Scale & Support (Ongoing)
Model monitoring, retraining, cost optimization, and expansion of successful patterns across the organization. Available as project or retainer.
