Our 5-Phase Delivery Process

Average time from kick-off to production-ready environment: 6–10 weeks. No slide decks before delivery.

01
Cloud Readiness & Strategy (Weeks 1–2)
Current state assessment, cost modeling, workload prioritization, and target architecture selection with ROI forecast.
